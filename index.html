01 — Approach

The patterns that limit teams
are rarely visible to the teams
inside them.

Conventional leadership development works on skills and behaviors — the things you can see and measure. We work on the layer underneath: the relational field, the status dynamics, the nervous system responses that shape how a team thinks before it knows it's thinking.

Our methodology integrates somatic intelligence, Enneagram typology, relational coordination theory, and systems thinking into a single coherent practice. Not as a toolkit. As a lens.

We call this operating below the line. Most teams live above it — performing, managing impressions, solving the presenting problem. Below the line is where the actual work lives: the fears underneath the conflict, the identity beneath the strategy, the body that knows before the mind does.

Our clients are leadership teams in tech, media, and impact sectors who sense that something structural is getting in the way — and are ready to find out what it actually is.

02 — About

Allan Silva —
executive coach,
leadership consultant.

Allan Silva is the founder of firstprinciples.team, a boutique leadership advisory practice based in Oakland, CA. He works with newly forming and existing leadership teams at the intersection of tech, media, and social impact.

His practice is built on two decades of work in organizational psychology, somatic healing, and leadership development — synthesized into a methodology that operates at the level of the relational field, not just the individuals within it.

Allan holds an Integral Coaching certification from New Ventures West and advanced training in Relational Somatic Healing. He is a certified Enneagram practitioner and practitioner of Chen Style Taijiquan.
